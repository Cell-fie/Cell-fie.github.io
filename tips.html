Tips & Tricks

Here are some tips to help you get better results in your analysis.

  1. If you have a well that you know has multiple cells use that well to adjust the focus before looking at other wells.
  2. If you are unsure if something is a cell or not zoom in to get a better look.
  3. Always make sure to check the edges well because it is often hard to see cells there.
